Family Background

Bankruptcy, bigamy, extramarital affairs, riots, forgery, imprisonment and deportation – as well as achievement, prosperity and artistic talent. Welcome to our family tree.

This site is a public companion to our private research database and WikiTree profiles. Each biography is generated from John’s genealogical research and original records, then polished into a readable life story for family and friends.

Our ancestry centres on four main branches:

  • Gilroy & Wilson (John’s Scottish lines). Agricultural workers and servants from Haddingtonshire and South Lanarkshire who moved into Scotland’s central belt as farming and industry mechanised.
  • Reynolds (Val’s paternal line). Merchants, clergy and farmers from Yorkshire, Northumberland and Herefordshire, many later based in London, with lines traced back to King Edward III.
  • Laws (Val’s maternal line). Agricultural labourers from Gloucestershire and Wiltshire, including family members transported to Australia after early 19th-century protests against agricultural mechanisation.

Living individuals are redacted on this public site. For our collaborative family tree (and to explore connections), please visit our family tree on WikiTree.
