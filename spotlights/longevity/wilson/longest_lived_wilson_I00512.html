Henry Hinshelwood

1831 - 1923

Henry Hinshelwood is the third great uncle of John Whitelaw Gilroy

In brief

Our longest lived male in the Wilson branch, Henry Hinshelwood, was the fourth son of James Hinshelwood and Elizabeth Dickson. One of 3 children, he was born on 8 December 1831 in Simpson's Court, Potterrow, Edinburgh, Scotland.

Henry married twice, these resulting in a total of 4 children.

He died on 24 June 1923 at Rockery Cottage, Dirleton, East Lothian, Scotland, from myocarditis, having lived for 91 years, 6 months, and 16 days.

Henry's surname has also appeared in sources with the spellings Hinchelwood, Henchelwood or Henshelwood.
