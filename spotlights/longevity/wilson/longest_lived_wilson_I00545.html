Jane Lamb

Jane Lamb is the second great aunt of John Whitelaw Gilroy

In brief

The longest lived person from John's maternal line (Wilson), Jane Lamb, was the first daughter of Alexander Lamb and Jane Cockburn. she was born on 17 April 1852 in Morebattle, Roxburghshire, Scotland.

Jane married James Penman Wilson on 21 Nov 1878 in Yetholm, Roxburghshire, Scotland and had 3 children.

She died on 31 December 1943 at Blackcastle, Crichton, Midlothian, Scotland, from myocarditis, general arterial sclerosis, senility, having lived for 91 years, 8 months, and 14 days.
