Jane Lamb

1852 - 1943

Jane Lamb is the second great aunt of John Whitelaw Gilroy

In brief

Our longest lived relation in the Wilson branch, Jane Lamb, was the first daughter of Alexander Lamb and Jane Cockburn. she was born on 17 April 1852 in Morebattle, Roxburghshire, Scotland.

Jane married once, these resulting in a total of 3 children.

She died on 31 December 1943 at Blackcastle, Crichton, Midlothian, Scotland, from myocarditis, general arterial sclerosis, senility, having lived for 91 years, 8 months, and 14 days.
