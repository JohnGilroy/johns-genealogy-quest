In brief

The longest lived person from Val's maternal line (Law), Percival Sidney Law, was the tenth son of James Thomas Law and Fanny Miles. One of 10 children, he was born on 12 August 1903 in Horcutt, Kempsford, Gloucestershire, England.

He never married.

He died on 1 December 1984 at 6 Victory Villas, Fairford, Gloucestershire, England, having lived for 81 years, 3 months, and 19 days.

Life and times

Percival Sidney lived all his life within Gloucestershire, in Cirencester, Kempsford, and Fairford (1903–1984).

Percival Sidney mainly worked as farm labourer (1921–1939). He was also employed as gardeners labourer.
