In brief

The longest lived male from Chris's maternal line (Law), Percival Sidney Law, was the tenth son of James Thomas Law and Fanny Miles. One of 11 children, he was born on 12 August 1903 in Horcutt, Kempsford, Gloucestershire, England.

He never married.

He died on 1 December 1984 at 6 Victory Villas, Fairford, Gloucestershire, England, having lived for 81 years, 3 months, and 19 days.
