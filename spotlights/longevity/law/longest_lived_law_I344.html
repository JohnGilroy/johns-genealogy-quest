Fanny Miles

1864 - 1956

Fanny Miles is the great grandmother of Valerie Christine Reynolds

In brief

The longest lived person from Val's maternal line (Law), Fanny Miles, was the twelfth daughter of George Miles and Esther Neat. One of 12 children, she was born on 9 June 1864 in Southrop, Gloucestershire, England.

She married James Thomas Law on 24 Sep 1887 in Southrop, Gloucestershire, England and had 11 children.

She died on 29 April 1956 at St Andrews Hospital, Chippenham, Wiltshire, England, from arterial schlerosis, having lived for 91 years, 10 months, and 20 days.

Life and times

Fanny lived all her life within Gloucestershire, in Fairford, Southrop, and Kempsford (1864–1956).

Fanny worked as domestic servant (1881–1881).
