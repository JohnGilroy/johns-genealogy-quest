In brief

The longest lived person from Val's paternal line (Reynolds), Ernest William Anthony Reynolds, was the third son of Reginald Hugh Reynolds and Marjorie Anne King. One of 3 children, he was born on 21 March 1921 in the 13 Gardenia Road, Bush Hill Park, Edmonton, Middlesex, England.

Tony was adopted by Thomas Henry Curtis Hopkins and Lilian Catherine Emmeline Hopkins at Woodyard Cottage, Ashridge, Little Gaddesden, Hertfordshire, England on 9 Nov 1934. After the adoption, he became known as Antony Hopkins.

Ernest William Anthony married twice (married Murial A Purves on 30 Nov 1946 in London, England; married Beatrix Taylor on 30 Nov 2011). Together, these unions produced no known children.

He died on 6 May 2014 at Berkhamsted, Hertfordshire, England, having lived for 93 years, 1 month, and 15 days.
