Marjorie A King

1891–1991

Marjorie Anne King is the grandmother of Valerie Christine Reynolds

In brief

The longest lived female from Chris's paternal line (Reynolds), Marjorie Anne King, was the only known daughter of William King. she was born on 30 May 1891.

She married Reginald Hugh Reynolds on 12 May 1915 in St George, Hanover Square, London, England and had 4 children.

She died on 18 July 1991 at Flat 1, 68 Coolinge Road, Folkstone, Kent, England, having lived for 100 years, 1 month, and 18 days.

We know next to nothing of Marjorie's life before she married husband Hugh. What we do know is summarised in the eulogy delivered by son Peter at her funeral. To date we have been unable to trace a birth record or census record prior to her marriage.

Life and times

Marjorie Anne lived in both England and Italy. In England, she lived in the counties of Berkshire, Hertfordshire, Isle of Wight, Kent, London, Norfolk, and Surrey. In Italy, she lived in the county of Gulf of Genoa, in Nervi.
