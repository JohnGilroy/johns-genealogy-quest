Ernest William Anthony Reynolds, CBE is the uncle of Valerie Christine Reynolds

In brief

The longest lived male from Chris's paternal line (Reynolds), Ernest William Anthony "Tony" Reynolds, was the third son of Reginald Hugh Reynolds and Marjorie Anne King. One of 4 children, he was born on 21 March 1921 in 13 Gardenia Road, Bush Hill Park, Edmonton, Middlesex, England.

Tony was adopted by Thomas Henry Curtis Hopkins and Lilian Catherine Emmeline Hopkins at Woodyard Cottage, Ashridge, Little Gaddesden, Hertfordshire, England on 9 Nov 1934. After the adoption, he became known as Antony Hopkins.

He married twice (married Murial A Purves on 30 Nov 1946 in London, England; married Beatrix Taylor on 30 Nov 2011). Together, these unions produced no known children.

He died on 6 May 2014 at Berkhamsted, Hertfordshire, England, having lived for 93 years, 1 month, and 15 days.

Tony was a well-known composer, conductor, lecturer, broadcaster, pianist, and author. He was appointed CBE in 1976
