Marjorie Anne King

1891 - 1991

Marjorie Anne King is the grandmother of Valerie Christine Reynolds

In brief

Our longest lived relation in the Reynolds branch, Marjorie Anne King, was the first daughter of William King. she was born on 30 May 1891.

Marjorie Anne married once, these resulting in a total of 4 children.

She died on 18 July 1991 at Flat 1, 68 Coolinge Road, Folkstone, Kent, England, having lived for 100 years, 1 month, and 18 days.

We know next to nothing of Marjorie's life before she married husband Hugh. What we do know is summarised in the eulogy delivered by son Peter at her funeral. To date we have been unable to trace a birth record or census record prior to her marriage.
