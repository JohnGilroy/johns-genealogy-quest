Marion Gray Whitelaw

1866 - 1966

Marion Gray Whitelaw is the second cousin of Caroline Mathieson Whitelaw, who is the grandmother of John Whitelaw Gilroy

In brief

The longest lived person from John's paternal line (Gilroy), Marion Gray Whitelaw, was the first child and eldest daughter of William Maltman Whitelaw, a master grocer, and Mary Gray. One of 3 children, she was born on 29 May 1866 in the High Street, Haddington, Haddingtonshire, Scotland.

She never married.

She died on 20 December 1966 at Birkenward Eventide Home, Skelmorlie, Ayrshire, Scotland, from generalised atherissclerosis with myocardial and cerebral degeneration, having lived for 100 years, 6 months, and 21 days.

Marion Grey Whitelaw was a member of a prosperous grocer's family who traded on Haddington's High Street from the 1860s until at least 1911. Marion was never recorded as having worked in the business, despite the fact that the rest of her family did.
