Leslie Widdowson is the uncle of John Whitelaw Gilroy

In brief

The longest lived male from John's paternal line (Gilroy), Leslie Widdowson, was the only known son of Harry Widdowson and Annie E Hassall. he was born on 5 March 1921 in Castle Donington, Leicestershire, England.

He married Rosetta Hannah Gilroy on 17 Sep 1945 in The Register Office, St Pancras, London, England and had 2 children.

He died on 21 September 2011 at Kegworth, Leicestershire, England, having lived for 90 years, 6 months, and 16 days.
